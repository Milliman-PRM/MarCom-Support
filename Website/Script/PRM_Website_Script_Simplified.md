##Solutions/Tools

###Care Coordinator Report
Provides care managers with a comprehensive view of recent patient clinical and claims activity, making management more efficient and effective. This allows for an entity to strategically select and manage patients for the best opportunity to reduce risk.

#####Features
 * Predicted estimates of a patient’s healthcare expenses and utilization over the next 6 months including probability of inpatient admission and emergency room visits 
 * 24 months of historical claims data including inpatient admissions, outpatient visits by service and provider, and prescription drugs
 * Identifies chronic conditions and associated risk factors
 * Total healthcare expense distribution and predicted potentially avoidable costs
 * Rankings that are based upon how your specific healthcare system has historically provided ambulatory care management services to its chronically ill patients
 * Identifies high ER users and low PCP users

#####Benefits
 * Provides risk-bearing organizations with the information needed to make more informed care coordination efforts
 * Identifies opportunities for care management improvement
 * Provides care managers with actionable data to easily select patient’s for further care intervention
 * Assembles the components necessary to impact patient decision making in a positive way
 * Offers the ability to understand the clinical and nonclinical drivers of potentially avoidable expenses for each patient
 * Custom analytics that will help meet healthcare challenges head on 
 * Supports self-service management of role-based security of PHI data

###Caregiver's Checklist
Provides physicians with a list of items to go over at the point of care.

#####Features
 * Quality Metrics that have or have not been met  
 * Identifies chronic conditions and associated risk factors
 * Patient's current conditions
 * Specialist visits
 * Prescription drug use

#####Benefits
 * Provides caregiver's with actionable data at the point of care

###Power User Datamart
A replication of the curated claims data delivered via a data mart transferred as a SQLite database. The central data repository consolidates data from a variety of clinical sources into a clinical data repository, presenting a unified view of a single patient. 

#####Features
 * Streamlined simple and advanced data preparation 
 * Data exchange flexibility
 * Interoperable functionality with other data repositories and systems
 * Industry expertise enables ability to leverage multiple data sources
 * New and informative data points can be created from existing data
 * Combines patient care and clinical information with insurance claims data
 * Processed customer data and Milliman proprietary analytics are extracted, in the form of flat files, from the PRM internal database 

#####Benefits
 * Consolidated data from a variety of clinical sources present a unified view of a single patient
 * Provides ability to understand whether the programs and patient contact statistically impact healthcare expenses 
 * File extracts can be loaded into customer’s data analysis systems
 * Provides the advantage of supplementing the core data stack underlying the predictive analytics, inclusion of clinical data, and new discoveries

###Cost Model Dashboard
A financial utilization tracking tool to identify trends in data and spending utilization patterns. 

#####Features
 * Ability to display information limited to certain cost model groups (Inpatient, Skilled Nursing Facility, Outpatient, Professional, Other, Additional) or cost model lines
 * Identifies cost and utilization trends in total and in part for sub segments of the population and/or sub segments of the services
 * Ability to segment data to only potentially avoidable cost or non-potentially avoidable costs

#####Benefits
 * Provides ability to quickly identify trends in data and  enrollment 
 * Provides ability to visualize and drill into claims or demographic data at desired population or service level 
 * Provides high level of granularity into utilization 
 * Provides ability to filter the population in order to display information at the segment level
 * Ability to compare cost and utilization among sub segments of a population
 * Ability to identify in-network vs. out of network utilization 

###Physician Performance
A single report providing each PCP’s performance measured against customer defined benchmarks such as PMPM, historic potentially avoidable healthcare expenses, inpatient admission rates, emergency room admission rates, and skilled nursing home admission rates (for aged managed populations). The physician performance analysis is a detailed report summarizing performance information for each individual PCP.

#####Features
 * Adjusts physician experience for patient morbidity using industry standard risk scores
 * Identifies PCPs that may be outliers within the physician population
 * Summarizes performance information for each individual PCP
 * Analyzes physician performance on an individual patient level

#####Benefits
 * Complete comparison of all physicians, as well as, detailed view of specific physician all in one place
 * Identify performance challenges of the clinicians associated systems 
 * Straightforward view of physician performance drivers
 * Easy identification of individual patients attributed to the PCP 
 * Findings allow for a data driven discussion with practicing PCPs

###Benchmarking
Ability to compare desired population against established cost and utilization industry standards or within own population.

#####Features
 * Ability to identify industry standard cost and utilization
 * Ability to customize performance targets
 * Ability to compare performance among population sub segments  
 * Provides comparisons at the service category level  

#####Benefits
 * Ability to identify well managed and loosely managed targets
